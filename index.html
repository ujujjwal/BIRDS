ABOUT US

BIRDS is a literary society of Raj Kumar Goel Instiute of technology.We needed a society to have a positive lasting impact on the surrounding community - analyzing policy, helping to spread inspiring ideas, promoting diversity and freedom of speech, but especially empowering students with the tools they needed to bring out their hesitation from them. BIRDS was established to encourage orating skills and overall personality development of students of Raj Kumar Goel Institute of Technology, Ghaziabad by a group of RKGITians they are now mentors for us. Sourav Rai, Shashank Bindal, Vatsalya Dubey, Nimisha Dwivedi, Mohit Sahu are the names of those mentors. We are proud to work efficiently ever since our establishment in Sep 2015.
